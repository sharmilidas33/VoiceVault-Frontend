Affordable Mental Health Care

by Jane Doe Started on May 15, 2025

About this petition

Mental health care is a fundamental right, yet millions of people struggle to access affordable and quality services. The rising cost of therapy, counseling, and medication makes it difficult for many individuals to get the help they need.

We are calling for:

  • Government-funded mental health programs to make therapy and counseling accessible for all
  • Lower prescription drug prices for mental health medications
  • More mental health professionals in underserved communities
  • Increased awareness and support for mental health issues in schools and workplaces

By signing this petition, you are advocating for better access to mental health services and ensuring that no one is left behind due to financial barriers. Every voice matters in making mental health care accessible for everyone.
